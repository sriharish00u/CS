Our Vision

The College works with a vision of providing quality higher education to all the sections of the society

Our Mission

The institution aims at providing quality education to the students of the downtrodden and weaker sections of the society, combined with a value based education, to ensure that they become ideal citizens of the country. The skills needed to meet the global challenges are to be imparted.
