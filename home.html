Department of Computer Science

Welcome to the Future of Innovation

The Department of Computer Science is a vibrant hub of learning, discovery, and innovation. Established with a vision to nurture problem-solvers, innovators, and global leaders in technology, our department blends strong academic foundations with hands-on learning to prepare students for the ever-evolving digital world.

Key Highlights

  • State-of-the-art Laboratories: Advanced computing labs equipped with the latest hardware, software, and high-performance servers, fostering hands-on experiments and projects.
  • Research & Innovation: Active research groups pursuing work in AI, Data Science, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, and Software Engineering.
  • Student Achievements: Consistent recognition in hackathons, coding competitions, and research conferences at national and international levels.
  • Industry Collaborations: Partnerships with leading tech companies, enabling internships, industry-driven projects, and career pathways.
  • Professional Growth: Student clubs, coding societies, and workshops that promote peer learning, teamwork, and leadership development.
  • Holistic Development: Beyond academics, students engage in cultural, social, and entrepreneurial activities to enhance personal and professional growth.
