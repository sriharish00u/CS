Department of Computer Science

The Computer Science Department offers a range of programs that prepare students for careers and research in cutting-edge technology fields. Below is a detailed overview of each program, including eligibility, duration, syllabus highlights, and future opportunities.

Programs Offered

B.Sc Computer Science

  • Eligibility:Candidates must have passed 10+2 (Higher Secondary) with Mathematics as a compulsory subject.
  • Duration:3 years (full-time), divided into 6 semesters.
  • Syllabus Overview:
    • Core subjects include Programming Languages (C, C++, Java), Data Structures, Computer Organization, Database Management Systems, Operating Systems, Software Engineering, Computer Networks, Web Technologies
    • Allied courses in Mathematics, Statistics, and Electronics.
    • Practical training in modern labs with the latest hardware/software, major/minor project work, and industry exposure.
  • Career Opportunities:
    • Roles in IT Services, Software Development, Web and Mobile App Development, Database Administration, Network Security, Data Analytics, E-commerce, Testing, and System Administration.
    • Graduates are recruited by top firms including Infosys, TCS, Wipro, government IT departments, and receive placements through campus job fairs.

M.Sc Computer Science

  • Eligibility: Graduation in Computer Science, Information Technology, or equivalent (UG degree).
  • Duration: 2 years (full-time), comprising 4 semesters.
  • Syllabus Overview:
    • Advanced Programming, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Advanced Database Systems, Research Methodology, Data Mining, Cloud Computing, and Emerging Technologies.
    • Hands-on research, dissertation, and industry collaborations.
  • Career Opportunities:
  • Higher-level positions in IT companies, software architecture, technical consulting, research and development, academia, government, and public sector organizations
  • Pathway to PhD or research fellowships.

M.Phil & Ph.D. in Computer Science

  • Eligibility: M.Sc degree in Computer Science or relevant field for M.Phil; M.Phil for Ph.D. (entrance may be required).
  • Duration:Duration: M.Phil – 1 year (full-time/part-time); Ph.D. – minimum 3 years (full-time/part-time).
  • Syllabus/Research Areas:
    • Specialized subjects in research methodology, computer networks, network security, artificial intelligence, image/data processing, cloud computing, and software quality management.
    • Original research projects under faculty guidance, with opportunities to publish and present at conferences.
  • Career Opportunities:Academic teaching positions, senior research roles, industry R&D, government consultancies, and startup leadership.

Additional Highlights

  • Low Fees & Scholarships:Government fees structure, substantial merit and need-based scholarships, and free hostel options for eligible students.
  • Industry Engagement:: Frequent workshops, seminars, and skill development events with industry experts from leading companies; active placement cell ensures regular job fairs and internships.
  • Skill Enhancement:Courses include soft skill development, resume building workshops, and interview training to maximize employability.

Explore diverse career pathways—from programming and analytics to research and entrepreneurship—with a degree from the Computer Science Department. Students graduate with both technical knowledge and practical skills, ready to meet the demands of a global technology environment.
